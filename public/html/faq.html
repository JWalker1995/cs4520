Power Newser : Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for articles?

To search for an article, go to the homepage here, and type the subject you want to search for in the textbox under the words "Power Newser". Then, click the blue "Search" button.


How do I add an article to my watchlist?

To add an article to the watchlist, click "add to watchlist above the article".


How do I remove an article from the watchlist?

Click "View Watched Articles" from the bottom of the page. A popup window will appear.
Then click "Remove from Watchlist" above the article.


What news sites do the articles come from?

The articles come from a variety of news sites using an API from Google.
